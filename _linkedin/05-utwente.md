I'm excited to share that I'll soon be joining the University of Twente and Medisch Spectrum Twente as a Postdoctoral Researcher! 🎓✨ 

I'll be working on the ZonMw-funded AI-HCC project, developing explainable AI systems for early detection of hepatocellular carcinoma through multi-modal data integration, combining longitudinal MRI imaging, multi-omics profiles, and clinical variables. 🧬🤖

I'm particularly excited to collaborate with Prof. Maryam Amir Haeri, Prof. Stéphanie van den Berg 🟥, and Dr. Maureen Guichelaar and the clinical team at Medisch Spectrum Twente on this important research that bridges AI innovation with real-world clinical impact. 🏥💡 

This aligns with my vision: developing advanced AI approaches that enable privacy-preserving collaboration across institutions while translating cutting-edge algorithms into clinically actionable insights for precision medicine. 🔬🌐
